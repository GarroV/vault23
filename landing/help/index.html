Vault23 Help & Reference

Help Center

Vault23 is your Telegram-based workspace for tasks, notes, reminders, and contractors. Everything is saved to your private workspace — just type or use the commands below.

Getting Started
/start Register and activate your workspace. Run this once when you first open the bot.
/help Show a quick command reference inside the bot.
/language Switch the bot language between Russian and English.
/stats Show your workspace summary — open tasks, notes count, pending reminders.
Tasks
/task Create a new task. The bot will ask for a title; you can also just type naturally and it will recognize the task intent.
/tasks List your open and in-progress tasks with inline action buttons.
/done Mark a task as completed. You'll get a numbered list to pick from.
/search Full-text search across all your tasks and notes.
Notes
/note Save a quick note. The bot stores anything you send after the command.
/notes Browse your recent notes with pagination.
Reminders
/remind Set a reminder. Example: /remind Call client tomorrow at 10:00. Natural-language date parsing supported.
/reminders List all pending reminders. Tap one to cancel it.
Contractors & Services
/contractor Add a new contractor (person or company) to your workspace directory.
/contractors List all contractors with their contact info.
/find Search contractors by name or specialization.
/addservice Add a service (price position) to your pricelist. Enter name, price, and optionally link to a contractor.
/services List your pricelist, grouped by contractor.
Knowledge Base
/kb Open the knowledge base menu — search, add, or list articles.
Integrations
/google Connect your Google account to sync with Google Calendar and Drive.
/email Send emails directly from the bot using your configured email address.
Account & Privacy
/subscription View your current plan, usage limits, and billing details.
/deletedata Permanently delete your workspace and all data. This action is irreversible — you will be asked to confirm by typing DELETE.
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add team members to my workspace?
Share your workspace invite link (available via /subscription). Anyone who joins through that link is added to your workspace and can create tasks and notes visible to the whole team.
Are my tasks and notes private?
Yes. Each workspace is fully isolated — other workspaces cannot see your data. Within a workspace, all members share the same data. If you want to keep something private, create a separate personal workspace.
What happens when my trial ends?
Your workspace moves to the free tier automatically. You keep access to all your data and core features. Paid features (voice processing, email sending) will be paused until you subscribe.
Can I export my data?
You can use /pricelist to generate a printable PDF of your services. Full data export is on the roadmap. Until then, contact support and we'll send you a JSON dump within 48 hours.
How do I delete my account?
Send /deletedata to the bot. After confirmation, your entire workspace — tasks, notes, reminders, contractors, files — is permanently erased. This cannot be undone.
My reminder didn't fire — what happened?
Reminders are processed every minute. If a reminder was missed, check that your workspace is active (not suspended). All times are stored in UTC — make sure the time you set matches UTC, not your local timezone (timezone conversion is a planned feature).
How do I get the pricelist PDF?
After adding services with /addservice, open your personal cabinet at the link in your bot profile. The Pricelist page generates a print-ready page you can save as PDF from your browser (File → Print → Save as PDF).
